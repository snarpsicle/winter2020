Refined Buffer

This extraction proved difficult due to trees that had similar characteristics to the power lines. To further the difficulty of this project the power lines in the LAS were of varying height. Where one power line's height ended another began. Trees within this range remain in the analysis. A power line vector was brought into the analysis to create the refined with buffer layer. This buffer is a 2 foot radius of each individual power line. Click the eye in the layer list to toggle on/off layers to better understand the refined buffer.
